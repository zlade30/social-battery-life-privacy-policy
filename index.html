Privacy Policy for Social Battery

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Summary: Social Battery stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Social Battery does not require an account, email address, phone number, or any form of registration.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your first name (used for the greeting on the home screen)
  • Your personality profile survey answers
  • Activity logs (activity type, duration, intensity, timestamp, and battery impact)
  • Your current battery level
  • App preferences (notification settings, daily reminder time)
  • Custom activity definitions you create
  • Customizations to built-in activity types

2. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). No data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Activity history is automatically capped at the most recent 200 entries. Older entries are discarded as new ones are added.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No email addresses or passwords
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reports sent to external services

4. Notifications

Social Battery may request permission to send local notifications for daily log reminders and automatic sleep logging. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No push notification server is involved, and no notification data is transmitted externally.

5. In-App Purchases

Social Battery may offer optional subscriptions through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by the respective platform. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing address.

6. Third-Party Services

Social Battery does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party services involved are the Apple App Store and Google Play Store for distribution and in-app purchase processing, which are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Children's Privacy

Social Battery does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • Reset within the app: Go to Settings and tap "Reset Onboarding" to erase all stored data.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app from your device permanently deletes all associated data.

There is no server-side data to request deletion of because no data ever leaves your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
