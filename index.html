Privacy Policy for Social Battery

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Summary: Social Battery stores all your data locally on your device, including wellness-related information you choose to log (for example social situations, sleep, and exercise, plus any custom activity types you create). We do not transmit or share that data with anyone. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Social Battery does not require an account, email address, phone number, or any form of registration.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your first name (used for the greeting on the home screen)
  • Your personality profile survey answers
  • Activity logs (activity type, duration, how draining or recharging it felt on a 1-5 scale, timestamp, and battery impact)
  • Your current battery level
  • App preferences (notification settings, daily reminder time)
  • Custom activity definitions you create
  • Customizations to built-in activity types

2. Health & Wellness Data

Social Battery allows you to log activities that may constitute health or wellness data, including:

  • Social and daily activities — built-in types such as one-on-one time, small groups, parties, meetings, and family time, including duration and how draining or recharging each session felt
  • Sleep — duration of sleep sessions you log
  • Exercise — duration and how recharging or draining exercise felt when you log it
  • Custom activities — labels and settings for activity types you add yourself; logs using those types are stored like any other entry
  • Social energy level — your current “battery” percentage, a self-reported mental wellness metric
  • Personality profile — your introvert/ambivert/extrovert classification based on onboarding survey answers

This data is entered entirely by you. The app does not read from Apple Health, Google Fit, or any other health platform. No sensors, wearables, or third-party health APIs are used.

This data is used exclusively to calculate your in-app energy meter and generate personal insights. It is stored only on your device using local storage and is never transmitted to any server, shared with any third party, or used for any purpose other than the features you see in the app.

3. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). No data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

Activity history is automatically capped at the most recent 200 entries. Older entries are discarded as new ones are added.

If you back up your device using Apple iCloud or Google backup services, your device provider may include app data in those backups based on your personal backup settings.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No email addresses or passwords
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No photos, camera, or microphone access
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reports sent to external services

5. Notifications

Social Battery may request permission to send local notifications for:

  • Daily log reminders — at the time you choose in Settings, if enabled
  • Free trial reminder — when you are in an active free trial, a one-time local reminder about your trial ending may be scheduled if notifications are enabled

These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No push notification server is involved, and no notification content is transmitted externally.

6. In-App Purchases

Social Battery may offer optional subscriptions through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by the respective platform. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing address.

7. Third-Party Services

Social Battery does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party services involved are the Apple App Store and Google Play Store for distribution and in-app purchase processing, which are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

Social Battery does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Because the app does not collect personal information from any user, there is no server-side child data for us to process or share.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it:

  • Reset within the app: Go to Settings and tap "Reset Onboarding" to erase all stored data.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app from your device permanently deletes all associated data.

There is no server-side data to request deletion of because no data ever leaves your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after the updated policy becomes effective means you accept the revised policy.
